Tariffa Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2024

Introduction

Tariffa is a Chrome extension designed to analyze product information and provide tariff-related insights. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our extension.

Information We Collect

Tariffa collects the following types of information:

  • Product Information: When you visit supported e-commerce websites, we analyze publicly available product information including:
    • Product names and descriptions
    • Prices
    • Country of origin information
  • Usage Data: We collect do not collect any usage information for this extension.

Third-Party Services

Tariffa uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI API: We use OpenAI's API to analyze product information and extract relevant details. The data sent to OpenAI is processed according to their privacy policy.
  • World Bank WITS API: We use the World Bank's WITS API to fetch tariff information. This data is publicly available and used according to their terms of service.

Data Storage

Tariffa:

  • Does not store any personal information
  • Will use Chrome's local storage to temporarily cache analysis results
  • Does not maintain any external databases of user data

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • All API communications are encrypted using HTTPS
  • We do not collect or store sensitive personal information
  • Analysis is performed locally in your browser when possible

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Uninstall the extension at any time
  • Clear all stored data through Chrome's extension settings
  • Contact us with any privacy-related concerns

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
